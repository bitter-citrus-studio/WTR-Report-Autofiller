## How to use (In testing)

1. On the main page, click on the zip file.
   <img width="944" height="444" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/955ce080-9951-45c2-bd73-50ea4be04752" />
2. On that page, hit the downlaod raw button which is at the far right of the page.
   <img width="816" height="239" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d4440f71-bdb7-450b-bd36-9d316c6a6f64" />  

3. Navigate to extension in Google Chrome in the top left (Will look like a jigsaw puzzle piece)
<img width="342" height="128" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/82c4b68f-aa6e-42d4-9c8a-ad474c7071f4" />

4. Click "Manage Extensions" at the bottom of the window
5. In the newly opened tab, turn on developer mode, which is in the top left. Turn on developer mode in Google Chrome Extensions. You should see new parts to the page which are circled in red:
   <img width="2004" height="627" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1f7e4383-63e8-4296-8f8b-bebbd4e14670" />
   
6. Unzip the downloaded folder, click load packed and Upload the whole unzipped folder
7. Extension will be available in the extension tab
