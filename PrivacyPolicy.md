<h1>SRU Report Autofiller Privacy Policy</h1>

<p><strong>Effective Date:</strong> 04/11/2025</p>

<h2>1. Data Collection</h2>
<p>SRU Report Autofiller does <strong>not collect, store, or transmit any personal data</strong> to external servers. The extension only reads form fields on the web page you are actively viewing to autofill them with the values you provide in the extension popup.</p>

<h2>2. How Data Is Used</h2>
<p>All data is processed <strong>locally within your browser</strong>. The extension uses the data solely to fill in form fields on the current webpage and does <strong>not save it beyond your browsing session</strong>.</p>

<h2>3. Sharing and Third Parties</h2>
<p>SRU Report Autofiller <strong>does not share any data</strong> with third parties. No personal information is transmitted outside your device.</p>

<h2>4. Permissions</h2>
<p>The extension requests permissions such as:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>activeTab </strong> – to detect the active page and inject autofill scripts.</li>
  <li><strong>Host permissions</strong> – to access the specific websites where forms are filled.</li>
  <li><strong>storage</strong> – only to save user preferences locally, if applicable.</li>
</ul>
<p>These permissions are used exclusively to provide the autofill functionality described above.</p>

<h2>5. Your Control</h2>
<p>All actions occur within your browser. You can disable or remove the extension at any time. No data leaves your device unless you explicitly save it yourself.</p>
